Newsletter Editorial Strategy improves go-to-market execution by tying audience, message, and production workflow to a specific business goal.

## Core Principles

- Message clarity starts with audience clarity.
- Distribution needs to be designed, not assumed.
- Content systems improve when reuse is intentional.

## Workflow

1. Define the target segment, outcome, and narrative.
2. Plan assets, channels, and production deadlines.
3. Launch with tracking for reach and conversion.
4. Reuse what works and retire what does not.

## Common Mistakes

- Creating assets before deciding the audience.
- Measuring vanity metrics alone.
- Treating one-off wins as a repeatable system.
