Editorial Calendar Design improves go-to-market execution by aligning audience, message, production workflow, and measurement.

## Core Principles

- Message clarity starts with audience clarity.
- Distribution plans should exist before production begins.
- Reusable systems outperform one-off wins over time.

## Workflow

1. Define the target segment, narrative, and desired outcome.
2. Plan assets, owners, channels, and launch timing.
3. Ship with tracking that connects attention to business results.
4. Reuse the strongest patterns and retire low-signal work.

## Common Mistakes

- Producing assets before validating the message.
- Measuring vanity metrics without conversion context.
- Assuming a one-off campaign is a repeatable system.
