Journey Mapping Optimization helps product teams make better decisions by tying goals, evidence, and tradeoffs to a concrete operating rhythm.

## Core Principles

- Every decision should point to a measurable outcome.
- Tradeoffs create alignment only when they are written down.
- Follow-through matters as much as the decision itself.

## Workflow

1. Define the user problem, business goal, and constraints.
2. Surface the assumptions, tradeoffs, and decision criteria.
3. Choose owners, timing, and the smallest meaningful next step.
4. Review the result and adapt the operating model from evidence.

## Common Mistakes

- Confusing discussion volume with decision quality.
- Skipping written ownership and success measures.
- Optimizing for internal preference over user impact.
