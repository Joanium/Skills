Stakeholder Alignment helps teams make cross-functional decisions with clearer tradeoffs, evidence, and follow-through.

## Core Principles

- Decisions should connect to a measurable outcome.
- Alignment improves when tradeoffs are explicit.
- Review loops matter as much as the initial plan.

## Workflow

1. Define the goal, stakeholders, and constraints.
2. Surface tradeoffs, assumptions, and decision criteria.
3. Choose the next action with owners and timing.
4. Review results and adjust the operating model.

## Common Mistakes

- Confusing activity with progress.
- Skipping written decisions and ownership.
- Optimizing for local team preference over user value.
